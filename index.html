About Uojdata

Uojdata is a University of juba website where data about any given inqury is found. it is purposefully developed to easy data collection about a given topic. it is made by university of juba students during their preparation for AI day, 2025. The students who voluntarily did it, made it so, such that those who shall be carrying out research will easily get it. As technology advances those prioritized this task over busy schedules to have it ready on the table are highly appreciated and thanks prof. Dr.Felix for cautioning us. Thanks to the two students who made data colection and brought this website to life.
